The Music I Play

I play a Persian instrument called Santur. Santur is a hammered dulcimer of Iranian origin, which is traditionally made of walnut or other fine woods. The Persian Santur has 72 strings and is played with two light wooden mallets known as "Mezrab." It produces a rich, resonant sound that is central to Persian classical music.
